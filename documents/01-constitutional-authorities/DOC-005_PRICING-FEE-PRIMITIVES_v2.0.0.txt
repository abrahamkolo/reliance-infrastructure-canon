**ICC (Intraclass Correlation Coefficient)**: Statistical measure of inter-rater reliability (Document 4 context). Also: International Chamber of Commerce (arbitration context).

**Reverse Charge**: VAT/GST mechanism where buyer (not seller) self-assesses tax on cross-border B2B services.

**Payment-as-Contract-Acceptance**: Legal doctrine where fee payment constitutes binding acceptance of artifact terms without separate signature.
