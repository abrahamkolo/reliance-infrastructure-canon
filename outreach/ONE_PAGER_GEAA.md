# GEAA — Global Evidence Admissibility Authority

**Solution:** GEAA provides deterministic evidence admissibility certification. Binary output: ADMISSIBLE or NOT ADMISSIBLE under specified standards. Cryptographic proof chain eliminates authenticity disputes.

1. Evidence submitted with metadata per GEAA specification
2. SHA3-512 hash generated at submission
3. Chain of custody verified against CCOCP (Document 27) requirements
4. Binary admissibility determination issued
5. Determination independently verifiable without MW involvement
